About GEC Girls Hostel

The GEC Girls Hostel, Jagdalpur, provides a safe and comfortable stay for students. Our hostel is equipped with modern amenities, including 24/7 security, Wi-Fi, mess facilities, and recreational spaces. We aim to create a home-like environment for our students to focus on their studies while ensuring their well-being.

Rooms are allocated based on availability, and hostel rules ensure discipline and safety for all residents. The warden and staff are always available to address students' concerns.
